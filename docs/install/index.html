Install Bloom on Device nightly

A bedside baby tracker on the Sunton ESP32-2432S024R board (CYD 2.4" resistive). This page flashes the firmware to your device directly from the browser — no toolchain, no command line.

Build: __BUILD_SHA_SHORT__ · __BUILD_DATE__

Unsupported browser. The installer uses WebSerial, which is only available in Chrome, Edge, or Opera on a desktop computer. Open this page in one of those browsers to continue.

1 — Plug it in

Connect the board to your computer with a USB-C data cable. Power-only cables won't work. If your OS doesn't see the board, install the CH340 driver.

2 — Click install

Your browser doesn't support WebSerial. Try Chrome or Edge. WebSerial is disabled. Re-open this page over HTTPS.

A pop-up will ask you which serial port to use. Pick the one that appeared when you plugged the board in.

3 — Connect to WiFi

After flashing, the board reboots and creates a setup WiFi network called BloomSetup. From your phone:

  1. Connect to BloomSetup (no password).
  2. A setup page should open automatically. If it doesn't, open any browser and go to http://192.168.4.1.
  3. Pick your home WiFi, enter the password, tap Save.

The display reboots one more time, fetches the time and your local weather, and lands on the Stats screen. You're done.

Tips

  • Tap a card on the Stats screen to log a feed, diaper, or sleep.
  • Swipe with < and > to see today, yesterday, and two days ago.
  • Tap the x on any event twice (within 2 seconds) to delete it.
  • Held BOOT during power-on to re-open the WiFi setup network.
